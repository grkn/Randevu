Features of ChatBot Panel

Desgin, develop and operate your ChatBot using NLP, training, deployment and analytic features of ChatBot Panel.

NLP INTEGRATION

ChatBot Panel uses wit.ai as NLP base. You do not need extra integration just fill our wit.ai form. We handle the rest for you.

SUBJECT BASED INTENT MANAGEMENT

ChatBot Panel has a subject based intent management. You can group intents according to their subjects.
Then ChatBot will understand subject flow in the dialog and make wise suggestions.

ANSWERS AND FALLBACKS

You can define your answer as text, attachement, quick reply, generic template, list template or carousel and link it to the related intent. Do not forget to create fallback answers in case unexpected questions.

EMOJI HANDLING

ChatBot Panel has an emoji library to create response to emoji sent by the user. Emoji library is a unique feature for our platform.

CHATBOT TRAINING

Training of ChatBot very easy. Just verify, change or create a new intent for questions of user. You can be sure that number of questions in training section will be decrease day by day.

CHATBOT SETTINGS MANAGEMENT

In ChatBot Panel, you can control threshold to change perception of ChatBot, state subject and general fallback answer for misunderstood dialogs.

OMNICHANNEL CONTROL

You can publish your ChatBot with same experience to Facebook Messenger and WebChat.

ANALYTIC INTEGRATION

ChatBot Panel has easy integration with Chatbase. You just create a bot in Chatbase and paste its API Key to our page. Automatically your ChatBot analytics will be recorded by Chatbase without extra effort.

How To Create ChatBot

Using ChatBot Panel you can create your ChatBot without coding skills very quickly.

CREATE NLP CONNECTION

To develope your ChatBot start creating wit.ai app for NLP purposes from inside our platform.

CREATE SUBJECTS

Tagging intents by subjects is important to ChatBot conversation.

CREATE INTENTS

Intents are group of sentences/questions user use within conversation.

CREATE ANSWERS

Each intent need an answer that can be text / attachement / quick reply / generic template / list template / carousel. Emoji is one of other answer type.

CONFIGURE SETTINGS

General fallback answers, persistent menu, threshold should be configured for better experience.

CONNECT TO WEBCHAT

In ChatBot Panel, you can test your ChatBot than when ready bring it to your site.

CONNECT TO FACEBOOK

Fill the necessary information and deploy you ChatBot to your Facebook Page.

CONNECT TO CHATBASE

To follow your ChatBot performance, create an account in www.chatbase.com and copy your given API key to our platform.
